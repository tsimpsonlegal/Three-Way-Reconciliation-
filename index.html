Bank statement

Enter the ending balance from the trust account bank statement, then import the statement transactions (CSV export from your bank's website) or add them by hand. Deposits are positive; checks, wires out, and other withdrawals are negative.

QuickBooks trust ledger

In QuickBooks, run the trust bank account register / transaction detail report for the statement month and export it to CSV (or Excel, then save as CSV). Import it here. The ending balance is your book balance as of the statement date — it auto-fills if the export has a running-balance column, and you can type it directly.

Three-way reconciliation

The app matches bank and book transactions (by check number, then by amount and date), computes outstanding items, and compares the three balances. Everything below updates automatically from the data on the first three tabs.

Georgia Bar Rule 1.15 requires trust account records to be kept for six years — print or save the signed report each month.
